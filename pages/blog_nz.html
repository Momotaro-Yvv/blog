2019-2020 Gap Year in New Zealand

Momotaro, Dec 2 2021

The time I spend in New Zealand is one of the most special experiences and turing point of my lifetime.
For those who love nature and slow pace of life, it is abosolutely one of the best options for traveling and living. On the very first day I arrived New Zealand Auckland Airport, I bought an air ticket straight to Queenstown, and started my journey of exploration there. It was there I decide to give myself some extra time to figure things out rather than rush into any workplace I have no passion on.
It was also here in NZ I met my Mr.right, and together with him, we travelled to most places on north island(much more to explore on south island), helped on farms in exchange for accomodations and food, dog sitting Maru the naughty puppy, and waitting Lucy to give birth to seven Sky Terrier puppies on 2020 Christmas night!
